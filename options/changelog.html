Change Log

v1.0.0

To retrieve your CLI keys, click on your external AWS Account in SSO. Once you're logged in to the console, click on the extension's icon. You should see your credentials there.

Copy these credentials in the following file

Linux/MacOS - ~/.aws/credentials
Windows - C:/Users/{your_user's_name}/.aws/credentials

v1.0.1

Added support for persistent service worker so that the extension does not shut down automatically in some time.


v1.0.2

Fixed persistence of service worker to make the extension compatible with tools like Okta, OneLogin, etc.
